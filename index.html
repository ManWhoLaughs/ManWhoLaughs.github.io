Annotation

This is my small researchment, which is provided in the HSE semester project's boundaries. On the interactive map hidden behind this page you can see the results of processing, summation and research of criminal parameters of some different countries all over the world.

This issue can be interesting in migrational and touristical purposes, because crime level is important specification in process of choosing a country for a holiday travel or a temporary work place. Knowledge about criminal situation in country becomes very important when you are traveling with children or when you do not have enough money to live/stay in reach and safe regions of chosen country.

All represented information is based on official data, published by police authorities of different countries, and open statistics from similar researchments, collected mostly on knoema.com and simillar web sites.

Please, consider that my researchment is based on information of period between 2010 and 2015. So, in 2018 it can be not quite accurate and relevant for some countries. You may click on countries to see their rating.

I analyzed wide number of different crime types for each country, such as: homicide, kidnapping, burglary, housebraking, raping and e.t.c. All indicators were represented in two types: total number of incidents and incidents rate on ten thousand people. There was created a linear model for evaluating total crime rating based on obtained result. Serious crimes were evaluated with highest weights with the aim to distinguish countries with lots of pickpocketing incedents and countries where you will be more likely killed than robbed. As you can see, many countries are not highlighted on the map. It happens because I could not find enough reliable official information about them. Some of them do not publish their statistics for free access and some of them even do not have any trustable data.

All analyzed countries are divided into six groups by my own crime : "Very Good", "Good", "Normal", "Increased", "High" and "Very High". To read additional information about each group you can switch over tabs at the top.

Very Good

Criminal indices of these countries are extremely low. This means that countries from "Very Good" group have very powerful and effective police system and strict legislation. You can travel to these countries without any fear that you are likely to be robbed. It does not mean that you can leave your baggage open on the street, but criminal risks are tiny in comparison with average level all over the world.

It is noticeable that most of this group members are small and rather prosperous countries, situated on the east of Europe. Of all these countries, China stands out. Despite the huge territory and more than one billion of populations, it shows great crime indicators. It happens because of very strict and effective government system for combating crime and deep implementation of modern security technologies such as face recognition system.

However, some small island states also have very good safety index, but in their case it this situation is due to some peaceful cultural traits of small population, calm and passive life in isolation and moderate government political course.

The most frequent crime type in these countries is common theft: pickpocketing and burglary. Homicides, rapes and kidnapping are on the lowest position.

Good

"Good" countries is some kind of european standart of life quality. Here you can not take too much care about your and your property safety. Ofcourse, situation in deep-green regions is worse than in the "Very Good" group, but it is still very nice. Life in these countries can be named "comfortable" and "safe".

Main part of this group is concentrated in central and Northern Europe. It is absolutely obvious, that the most powerful and reach european countries have good criminal statistics, in the light of developed police system and high educational and cultural level of population. Countries of the Baltic States also have good parameters because of stable life and satisfying level of economical development.

However, in the recent years european situation was significantly worsened in the light of migrants and refugees from undeveloped countries. Thats why now crime indices of central Europe are keeping slightly grow up, but it is not so strongly expressed on my rating, because of earlier period of time.

The most frequent types of crime are burglary, housebraking and robbery. Also, reach countries have greater number of car thefts, because of greater total number of expensive cars.

Normal

The name "Normal" is pretty subjective, so far as normal level is measured by myself. But in fact, this is interlayer between the generally recognized safe countries and countries with some notable crime problems. This is the fair median. Many of these countries have insignificant differences with their neighbours in "Good" group, but do not have so well-working police system.

Life in these countries can not be characterized with some controversial points. Life safety is absolutely normal. Moreover, overall life standards in all of these countries may be extremely different. "Normal" rank means only satisfying crime level. It do not give any information about other spheres of life. But at least you may be calm about your life safety.

I suppose that normal indices may show either that you really do not have to worry about your safety here or just specially processed official crime data down to average level. However, this depends on each separately selected country.

Increased

Increased level leaves the borders of relatively safe life, but not too critical. All of orange regions are suitable for life and work. You should just pay more attention for your safety in some districts or special places and you will not feel the huge difference with "normal" level. This enlargement in offenses number may be connected with different reasons: imperfect laws, which do not restrict crime in the right scale, problems with migrants or just poverty.

The most notable representative of this class is USA. As everybody knows, it has one of the most powerful economies in the world and great carrer opportunities. But with such a great amount of resources and territories USA also has a list of various problems: migrants from South America and controversial law on the carrying of weapon.

From this example you can see that countries with "Increased" rank may possess significant advantages and some tangible problems. To build up a full picture you should learn more about particular country you want to visit or emigrate. These regions are contradictory in many apsects.

"Increased" category is simillar to "Normal" in crime types with differences in raising level of robbery and cars thefts.

High

Name "High" speaks for itself. "High" countries have significantly increased level of crime, more often they are located together with the other "High" and "Very High" ones. This category is fairly broad, country from it can be both "little bit more than slightly increased" and "extremely high crime level aspiring to the worst category". That is why you should consider each "High" country more detailed. Here numbers are most important.

Despite the large variance, all red countries are not recommended to accommodation. High crime level makes them not safe and not comfortable to simple workers. Your life and holding is not reliably protected here. Huge percent of crimes lays on serious types such as people thefts and homicides.

As mentioned earlier, most of these countries are located in huge groups. Africa and South America is two main centers. Countries with high crime level have similar problems: low standard of living, weak police system, low level of education and poverty. It is rather clear, that all these characteristics are not satisfying for work migration or long-live journey.

Very High

Mark "Very High" displays really deplorable situation in bright-red regions. It means that local police system absolutely can not cope with crime. Great influence on life in this countries is provided by large criminal groups, which are above the law. A characteristic feature of this countries is enormous traffic in drugs and illegal weapon-trade.

You should think twice before going to this countries, because level of homicides, rapes, kidnapping and people theft is out of bounds of acceptable level. If you plan to be out of the relatively safe touristical areas, you put yourself in danger, especially in the evening time. You should pay attention for your safety and carefully choose places of walking.

As you can see, most part of this group is located in South America. There are a lot of countries with very interesting and old culture, which are attractive for tourists. However, most of them have relatively safe touristic places, because tourism brings significant revenues to the government. And if you are looking for bright memories from Brazilian Carnival, for example, you may feel yourself little bit relaxed, but exploring native citizen life in work districts is strongly not recommended.

And if you have firmly decided to visit one of this countries, you should carefully choose place of your hotel or residence and search for reliable contacts with official and legal local companies.
